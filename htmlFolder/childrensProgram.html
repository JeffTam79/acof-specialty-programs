Overview

Through educational assistance, enrichment activities, and emotional well-being programs, we help children build confidence, succeed academically, and develop life skills for a brighter future.

IMG

Why childrens program matters

Stable housing is just the beginning, but children also need nurturing environments that support their academic, emotional, and social development, allowing them to learn, grow, and thrive. ACOF’s Children’s Programs are designed to address educational gaps, foster independence, and encourage social and emotional development through holistic, trauma-informed care, ensuring a strong foundation for lifelong success.

Our Partners In Empowing Youth

ACOF’s Children’s Programs thrive thanks to the collaboration of dedicated partner agencies and academic institutions that share our commitment to nurturing resilient, hopeful futures for children affected by homelessness and trauma.

These organizations provide mentorship, arts programming, educational support, and volunteers who enrich the lives of our youngest residents. By working together, we create a robust network of care that supports healing, creativity, and academic success.
